Penguin

You can find them in Antarctica and Antarctic islands, the Galapagos Islands off the coast of Ecuador, South Africa, New Zealand, Australia, Peru and Chile.

  • Scientific Name: Spheniscidae.
  • Lifespan: Up to 30 years but little blue penguins have the shortest lifespan of up to 6 years.
  • Average Length: Adults are about 1.1 m (3 ft 7 in).
  • Diet: Penguins eat krill, squids, and fishes.

They are black and white, some have orange and yellow beaks. When they are babies they are light gray. They live in groups all their lives.
